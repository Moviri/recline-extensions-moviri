<h4>Filter Actions - onHover</h4>
<p>
This example shows a NVD3 horizontal barchart linked to a virtualmodel (necessary for computing region totals) and a Slickgrid table linked to a filtered model containing the daily details of the revenues.<br>
<br>
When you hover on the chart, the slickgrid is filtered by region and shows the day-by-day data pertaining the selected bar only.
</p>
